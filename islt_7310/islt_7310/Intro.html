Hello World! My name is Michele Dempsey. I am a geometry teacher with 29 years of experience. I was not an outstanding math student. In fact, geometry was my biggest challenge. My high school geometry teacher saw me at a math convention. She was shocked! Many of my students complain about math. It is too difficult. My own struggles with math make me more empathetic and encouraging. I have learned tricks and shortcuts. I enjoy sharing my problem-solving strategies with others. I love watching people grow more confident. Sometimes, students even learn to enjoy math. Currently, I am finishing my degree in Educational Technology. I am learning how to use technology in my geometry class more effectively. I have created webpages in several other classes using templates. In this class, I hope to learn how to customize a website for my students. Outside of math class, I love running, cheeseburgers, and doughnuts.
